!!! abstract "Ingredients"
    - 1 large cauliflower  
    - 2 tbsp mustard  
    - 6 tbsp mayonnaise  
    - 2 tsp vinegar  

!!! tip "Utensils"
    - Pot for boiling  
    - Mixing bowl  
    - Spoon  

!!! info "Information"
    - Cost: $  
    - Prep & cook time: ~25 minutes

## Preparation Method

1. Cook the cauliflower until tender.  
2. Mix mustard, mayonnaise, and vinegar in a bowl.  
3. Pour sauce over cauliflower and serve.
